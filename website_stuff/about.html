This guide aims to outline the core values of NMH as they pertain to sustaining a clean, safe, and sanitary dining hall. In order to keep a clean and safe environment, everyone is responsible for returning all dishes and utensils to the dish room and cleaning the area in which they ate. If dishes are broken, please ask a Dining Hall employee for help to ensure the mess is adequately taken care of. Similarly, it is important to wash hands or use the hand sanitizers before touching utensils or dishes that will eventually be used by others. We do not reuse our plates- if you would like a second serving, please bus your dishes and get new ones to avoid cross-contamination. In addition, please do not lick your fingers then touch any dish or utensil. You must wait to begin eating until you sit down at your table, there is no eating at the bars or while standing in line. Use the tongs or serving utensils provided to serve yourself food- do not use your hands. Avoid touching your eyes, face, and mouth while getting food from the servery and cover your mouth when you cough.
